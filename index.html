TELENET - Agile Way Of Working ('TAWOW')
Netcracker 2021 contract – Teamed up with Vendor Management to rework the current Netcracker contracts into a new 'Agile' contract. Move away from the current Waterfall Way of Working ('WoW'), running workshops to understand the requirements of all stakeholders (Architecture / Development / Testing / Release Management / Operational Readiness / Operations), alignment on SLA’s and KPI’s, charges, introduce lessons learned from previous Vendor Management Meetings, introduce a Capacity Based Model.

TELENET - Popeye Programme
Managed the transition of 85+ Digital and Data resources and 35+ legacy BSS / OSS applications of several incumbents partners to two main strategic partners Infosys (for Development and Support) and Cognizant (for Testing). The programme also included the management of the University track, the Employee Value Proposition track, Communications to Key Stakeholders and setup of the Contract Governance. This programme aims to save 30+ mio€ CAPEX over 5 years. Reporting to the CTO and occasionally to the SLT of Telenet.
TELENET - Dependencies on Commercial Whole Sale ('Doc WHS') programme
BUILD part - Make sure that the historical dependencies of the BASE legacy environment (Ericsson components BSCS / BCS / CVAS, Epiphany...) on the BASE Retail environment are cleared and that a 'Chinese Wall' on the new inventories (Ninas / Dinoman) was implemented. Automation of the SIM Ordering process (towards and from Gemalto / Idemia). Migration of about 7 million IMSI's and MSISDN's to the new inventories. Java development on an Oracle WebLogic environment.

DECOMMISSIONING part - Realizing synergies for about 60 (mostly) BASE legacy CSS, BSS and OSS applications that are not needed anymore during and after the migration of Base Pre Paid, Post Paid and B2B customers to the new integrated Telenet / BASE IT stack (‘Origin’). This programme aimed to save 15 mio€ in TCO for the IT Transformation & Integration BU. Total CAPEX investment of 1.85 mio€.
